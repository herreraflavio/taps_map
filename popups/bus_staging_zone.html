Bellevue Lot Bus Staging Zone

The Bus Staging zone is located on the south side of Bellevue Lot. For a visual representation please visit here. Bus Staging zone is able to maintain up to 4 bus at a time. If Bus Staging zone is full, the bus must leave campus and return for pickup at a later time. For more informaiton please contact TAPS at (209) 228-8277 or visit here.
