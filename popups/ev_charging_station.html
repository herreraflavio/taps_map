Electric Vehicle Charging Station

These spaces provide access for charging purposes only.  When charging is complete, the vehicle must be moved to an alternate location as soon as possible. Please do not unplug other vehicles without permission from the vehicle owner and be sure to return the cord to the charging station when you are finished.
